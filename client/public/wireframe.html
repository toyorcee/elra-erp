Project Management Workflow

Project Types

  • Personal Projects
    • Created by any department member
    • Requires HOD approval
    • Limited budget threshold
  • Departmental Projects
    • Created by department HOD
    • Requires Finance review for budget > ₦500,000
    • Executive approval for high-value projects
  • External Projects
    • Created exclusively by HR HOD
    • Requires vendor details and project items
    • Mandatory compliance and regulatory checks
    • Executive approval required

Approval Workflow

1. Project Creation
2. Initial Review
3. Budget Allocation
4. Procurement Process
5. Implementation

Budget Thresholds & Approval Flow

Personal Projects

  • ≤ ₦1M:
    • No Budget Required: Auto-approved
    • Budget Required: Finance → Executive
  • ₦1M - ₦5M:
    • No Budget Required: Auto-approved
    • Finance Dept: Direct to Executive
    • Other Depts: Finance → Executive
  • > ₦5M:
    • No Budget Required: Auto-approved
    • Finance Dept: Direct to Executive
    • Other Depts: Finance → Executive (Higher Scrutiny)

Departmental Projects

  • ≤ ₦1M:
    • No Budget Required: Auto-approved
    • Budget Required: Finance → Executive
  • ₦1M - ₦5M:
    • No Budget Required: Auto-approved
    • Finance Dept: Direct to Executive
    • Other Depts: Finance → Executive
  • > ₦5M:
    • No Budget Required: Auto-approved
    • Finance Dept: Direct to Executive
    • Other Depts: Finance → Executive (Higher Scrutiny)

External Projects

  • ≤ ₦1M:
    • No Budget Required: Legal → Executive
    • Budget Required: Legal → Finance → Executive → Budget
  • ₦1M - ₦5M:
    • No Budget Required: Legal → Executive
    • Budget Required: Legal → Finance → Executive → Budget
  • > ₦5M:
    • No Budget Required: Legal → Executive
    • Budget Required: Legal → Finance → Executive → Budget (Higher Scrutiny)
Special Cases:
  • SUPER_ADMIN: All projects auto-approved
  • Finance Department: Skip finance approval
  • External Projects: Only HR HOD & SUPER_ADMIN can create
